# Wiicode.ma Brand & Design Guide

This guide contains all the essential design elements used in the Wiicode.ma website. Use these values to maintain consistency across social media posts, stories, and other marketing materials.

## 1. Color Palette

### Primary Colors (King Green)
Used for backgrounds, cards, and deep contrast.
- **King Green 950**: `#002416` (Main Background)
- **King Green 900**: `#013220`
- **King Green 800**: `#0b4630`
- **King Green 700**: `#155d42`
- **King Green 600**: `#1e7e5a`

### Accent Colors (Royal Gold)
Used for buttons, highlights, borders, and gradients.
- **Royal Gold 300**: `#e5c56d`
- **Royal Gold 400**: `#d4af37` (Primary Gold)
- **Royal Gold 500**: `#c5a059`
- **Royal Gold 600**: `#aa8c49`

### Neutral Colors
- **Text Light**: `#f3f4f6` (Gray 100/200 equivalent)
- **Text Muted**: `#9ca3af` (Gray 400 equivalent)
- **Glass Border**: `rgba(197, 160, 89, 0.15)`

## 2. Gradients

### Golden Text Gradient
Used for headlines and special emphasis text.
```css
background: linear-gradient(to right, #bf953f, #fcf6ba, #b38728, #fbf5b7, #aa771c);
```

### Glassmorphism (Card Style)
Used for cards, sections, and overlays.
```css
background: rgba(11, 70, 48, 0.3);
backdrop-filter: blur(16px);
border: 1px solid rgba(197, 160, 89, 0.15);
```

## 3. Typography

- **Font Family**: 'Cairo', sans-serif
- **Weights**: 
  - Light (300) for body text
  - Regular (400) for general text
  - SemiBold (600) for subtitles
  - Bold (700) for buttons
  - Black (900) for main headlines

## 4. Visual Elements

### Shapes & Blobs
- **Green Blob**: `#155d42` (King Green 700) with heavy blur
- **Gold Blob**: `#aa8c49` (Royal Gold 600) with heavy blur

### Icons
- **Library**: FontAwesome 6.4.0
- **Style**: Solid icons for actions, Brands for social media.

## 5. Usage Examples

**Instagram Story Background:**
Use `#002416` as the solid background. Add a blurred circle of `#155d42` in the top-left and `#aa8c49` in the bottom-right.

**Call to Action Button:**
Background: `#c5a059` (Royal Gold 500)
Text: `#000000` (Black)
Font: Cairo Bold

**Headline:**
White text, with key phrases using the **Golden Gradient**.

## 6. Brand Identity & Strategy

### Target Audience (Who are we talking to?)
- **Non-Technical Founders**: People with brilliant business ideas but zero coding skills.
- **Early-Stage Entrepreneurs**: Startups in the "Idea" or "Pre-Seed" phase needing an MVP.
- **SMEs in Morocco**: Small/Medium businesses looking to digitize their operations (Apps/Web).
- **Visionaries**: Individuals who want to build the next big thing but feel stuck by technology.

### Niche
- **MVP Development (Minimum Viable Product)**: We don't just build "websites"; we build *products* that launch businesses.
- **Technical Partnership**: We position ourselves as a "Partner" (CTO-as-a-Service), not just a freelancer or agency.

### Tone of Voice
- **Empowering & Reassuring**: "You focus on business, we handle the tech."
- **Professional & Premium**: High-quality service, reflected in the Gold/Green aesthetic.
- **Accessible (Moroccan Context)**: Uses a mix of professional Arabic/English and relatable "Darija" concepts (e.g., "Bda mchrou3ek", "Bla machakil").
- **Direct & Action-Oriented**: Focus on results (Launch, MVP, Growth) rather than technical jargon (Code, Servers, APIs).

### Content Pillars for AI Agents
1.  **Educational**: Explaining what an MVP is, why you shouldn't spend months building before launching.
2.  **Pain Points**: "Can't find a developer?", "Freelancers ghosted you?", "Too expensive?".
3.  **Social Proof**: Success stories, "We build fast".
4.  **Behind the Scenes**: Showing the "Magic" of turning an idea into code.
